APJ Abdul Kalam

Born: October 15, 1931

Died: July 27, 2015

"Dream, dream, dream.
Dreams transform into thoughts,
and thoughts result in action."

Biography: Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was a renowned Indian scientist, politician, and the 11th President of India. He played a key role in the nation's space and missile development programs and was a source of inspiration for millions. Despite his many achievements, Dr. Kalam remained humble and focused on inspiring the youth of the nation. He encouraged students to dream big and work hard to achieve their goals. Dr. Kalam's legacy continues to inspire generations to come.
